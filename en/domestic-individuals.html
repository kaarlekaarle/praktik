Family life and its ending

A prenuptial agreement can be drawn up before or during a marriage. It shapes the outcome of any future division of assets and determines what equalisation payment, if any, is owed. Once a divorce petition has been filed, we handle the preparation of the division and the negotiations. Where necessary, we represent one party in a court-administered division. Child custody, residence, visitation rights and maintenance are decisions that cannot easily be undone. We help make them well.

Inheritance and estate planning

Inheritance always means the loss of someone close. Disagreements about what was intended — and what wasn't — tend to surface at the worst possible moment. We draft wills, legacies and powers of attorney, and handle estate inventories and divisions. A plan made in good time keeps assets where they were meant to go and keeps inheritance tax from becoming a surprise.

Construction, residential and real estate transactions

Disputes over construction contracts, residential sales and real estate often come down to disagreements about what standard of quality was promised. Water damage, mould, structural defects, latent defects and legal encumbrances are all familiar territory for us. We represent buyers, sellers, contractors and clients in disputes arising from these transactions and assess your prospects for rescission or a price reduction.
